# <i class="fas fa-project-diagram section-icon" aria-hidden="true"></i> <span data-lang="en">Projects</span><span data-lang="zh">项目</span>

<div data-lang="en" markdown="1">

## Yubing: Youth AI Education and Innovation Practice Ecosystem

Yubing is an innovation ecosystem platform that starts from youth AI education and aims to build infrastructure for broader AI literacy, science-and-technology education, and innovation practice.

Through **AI + hardware, AI + video, AI + software, and open co-created courses**, the project connects universities, governments, companies, public-interest organizations, and developer communities.

The name “Yubing” comes from the phrase “夏虫不可语冰” in *Zhuangzi*, symbolizing the effort to break cognitive limits and make the seemingly impossible understandable, learnable, and creatable.

## Xu Xiake Digital Humanities Map Platform

A digital humanities platform based on *The Travels of Xu Xiake*, combining NLP, spatiotemporal narrative, and interactive visualization.

I worked on NLP, event extraction, backend development, and frontend platform construction. The related first-author work has been accepted by **CDH 2025** and **DH 2026**, and the platform has been registered as software copyright.

Link: [Gitee](https://gitee.com/wei-rain-light-dust/xu-xiake-visualization)

## Taikang Healthcare GraphRAG Question Answering System

An enterprise-oriented intelligent question answering system for healthcare, insurance, and elderly-care scenarios.

I led the design and implementation of a GraphRAG-based architecture that combines knowledge graph retrieval with LLM reasoning, using Neo4j, FastAPI, and Streamlit to explore reliable vertical-domain AI applications.

Link: [GitHub](https://github.com/Nelly-se/Knowledge-Graph-Construction)

</div>

<div data-lang="zh" markdown="1">

## 语冰：青少年 AI 科创教育与创新实践生态平台

语冰是一个以青少年人工智能科创教育为入口的创新生态平台，关注全民 AI 素养、科创教育与创新实践基础设施建设。

项目通过 **AI + 硬件、AI + 视频、AI + 软件、开源共创课程** 等方式，连接高校、政府、企业、公益组织与开发者网络，尝试构建面向青少年和更广泛人群的 AI 教育与创新实践生态。

项目名称取自《庄子·秋水》“夏虫不可语冰”，寓意打破认知局限，让看似不可能的事情变得可被理解、可被学习、可被创造。

## 《徐霞客游记》数字人文地图平台

基于《徐霞客游记》的数字人文可视化平台，结合自然语言处理、时空叙事和交互式可视化，将古籍文本转化为结构化事件与动态地图。

我负责 NLP、事件抽取、后端搭建与前端平台开发。基于该项目的方法与平台，一作论文已被 **CDH 2025** 与 **DH 2026** 接收，平台已登记软件著作权。

链接：[Gitee](https://gitee.com/wei-rain-light-dust/xu-xiake-visualization)

## 泰康医疗 GraphRAG 智能问答系统

面向医疗、保险与养老场景的企业级智能问答系统。

我负责整体方案设计与落地，基于 GraphRAG 架构结合知识图谱检索与大语言模型语义理解，使用 Neo4j、FastAPI 与 Streamlit 完成系统开发，探索知识图谱与大模型融合在垂直领域的落地方式。

链接：[GitHub](https://github.com/Nelly-se/Knowledge-Graph-Construction)
